# Product Overview

ScoreBase is the foundational platform for local sports leagues. It provides real-time game scores, team standings, schedules, and player statistics through an iOS mobile application.

## Core Value Proposition

"The Foundation of Local Sports" - ScoreBase enables sports fans to follow their favorite teams and players across multiple sports through an intuitive mobile interface with comprehensive data visualization.

## Target Users

- Youth sports league participants and families
- Adult league players and fans
- League administrators and scorekeepers
- Multi-sport league organizers

## Key Features

- Real-time live game scores and updates
- Team standings and rankings
- Game schedules with filtering
- Player statistics and leaderboards
- Team rosters and profiles
- Multi-sport support (basketball, soccer, hockey, baseball, football)

## Brand Characteristics

- Professional and structured
- Data-driven and event-focused
- Multi-sport neutral (no sport-specific bias)
- Modern infrastructure approach
- Scalable across leagues and regions

## Monetization Model

- Free tier: Basic features with 1 division
- Standard tier ($49-99/month): Full features for leagues
- Pro tier ($149-199/month): Advanced analytics and customization
- Revenue model targets leagues, not individual players
